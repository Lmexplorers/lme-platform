A JOURNEY WE'RE BUILDING TOGETHER 💗

I'm so excited to introduce you to Renate AI, an AI version of me that's here to support you with questions about Montessori pedagogy, the Montessori tradition, and how to best get started on your journey. This is something I've dreamed of building for a long time, and you're here with me from the very beginning. 🌷

Because Renate AI is in beta, this means:

  • We're testing, learning, and improving along the way
  • Small unexpected errors or hiccups may occur
  • I may need to adjust how things work as we figure out what's realistic

I want you to feel like you're part of this journey with me, like a behind-the-scenes friend helping me shape the future of LME. Feedback, ideas, and support from you will mean the world. 💗

💡 Fair usage

Renate AI is here to help you grow in your Montessori journey with clarity and calm. To ensure everyone has a good experience, we have some simple guidelines for use:

  • Use Renate AI for your own learning and personal development
  • Be mindful of how much you use it, so the system works well for all members (avoid excessive use)
  • Understand that we may occasionally need to set limits, pause, or adjust usage to keep everything fair and sustainable. This means Renate AI may sometimes be unavailable, or available only with limited functionality. We are not liable for losses or inconveniences related to the use and availability of Renate AI.

🚀 Why this matters

AI is powerful, but also resource-intensive. I want to be completely open with you that this is something I'm experimenting with. There are costs and unknown factors involved, so this policy helps us balance the magic of innovation with the reality of making this sustainable long-term.

🌟 My promise to you

I will always be honest with you. If we have to make changes, I'll explain why. If we discover something wonderful, you'll be the first to know. And above all, my highest priority is creating the best possible experience for you as you build your Montessori practice, your classroom, or your home.

So dive in, play, explore, and let Renate AI support you. Thank you so much for being part of this exciting experiment with me! 💗🌷
